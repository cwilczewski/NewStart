About

New Start is a Chrome extension designed to really let you customize your new tab experience by giving you control over what you see, and how you search, all within a clean UI. It's built with with the help of jQuery, Moment.js, Vue.js, and Fontawesome.

The images in this plugin are taken from Unsplash, Reddit, and Google Earth View, as well as some offline images provided by the developer himself.

Developed by: Chris Wilczewski
